The conference will take place at the Conference Centre and Training Centre of the Luxembourg Chamber of Commerce, located in Luxembourg-Kirchberg.

Luxembourg Chamber of Commerce
7, rue Alcide de Gasperi
L-2981 Luxembourg

How to access

By public transport

Luxembourg Chamber of Commerce is easily reachable by public transportation.
Remember that Luxembourg offers nationwide free public transport for everyone!

Bus

Stop Léon Hengen: lines 8, 20
Stop Jean Monnet: lines 1, 16, 18, 120, 144, 165, 172, 192, 194, 195, 213
Stop Europe: lines 155, 159, 184, 300, 303, 323

Tram

Line T1 : stop Coque (or Parlement Européen)

Train

Pfaffenthal Kirchberg station and Luxembourg central station are connected to the tram line.

More information on public transport (schedules, route, etc.) can be found on https://www.mobiliteit.lu/en/

By bike

A cycle path along Avenue John F. Kennedy connects the Kirchberg district to the city center and station center.
You can use the bicycle-sharing system vel'OH, several stations are located nearby the venue: Jean Monnet, Piscine Olympique, Europaparlament, Coque.

By car

The Kirchberg district is accessible by car from the city center or by the A1 motorway Exit 8 Luxembourg Centre - LUXEXPO Direction Quartier Européen Nord/Sud.

Several parking options are available:

  • Chamber of Commerce car park (reserved for visitors, subject to availability)
  • Public park Erasme
  • Public park Luxexpo
  • Public park Gernsback
  • Public park Bibliothèque nationale du Luxembourg
  • Public park Trois Glands
  • Public park Place de l'Europe Luxembourg 

By plane

Bus line 16 connects Luxembourg airport to the Kirchberg district in a few minutes (Jean Monnet stop).
